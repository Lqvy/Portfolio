About

How my interest started.

About me... I've had an interest in technology since I was young, I played lots of video games but never truly got into physical technology until my later years. Upon my first part-time job, I built my own computer, this kickstarted my true interest in the topic. From here, I later got interested in game indie dev'ing and 3D art.

Continued..

From my interest in indie dev came my interest in code. As I got older I wanted something I could do more 'professionally' but still retain a sense of constant learning and some creativity. So, out of the sea of things to choose from, Data Analytics arose. Data Analytics allows me to do deep dives into the understanding of processes and services within these companies, but still show some creativity through visualizations.

Education.

I was in traditional high school for about 2 years, then I was introduced to Polk State Lakeland Gateway to College Collegiate High School, in which I started college full-time at 16. I had my AA degree at 19 due to this and will have my Bachelors as of the middle of 2024 (21 y/o). I am to continue schooling into UCF for the Data Analytics program they provide to continue my education.

New Yet Defining Interests.

Through my time pursuing these ventures I've worked a lot on my mental health and more recently done lots of new things mixed with much more socializing. This led to my interest in backpacking, traveling, philosophy, and psychology. Philosophy has massively changed the way I approach coding, as philosophy is great for learning in depth critical thinking, similar to code. Psychology is of course great for personal growth, understanding the brain can change how you approach most situations.
